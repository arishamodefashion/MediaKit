About · Channel
ArishaModeFashion · The Channel
Every Video,
Every Frame —
A Model Stars
ArishaModeFashion is India's dedicated model-feature YouTube channel. Every single video we publish puts a model at the center — fashion walks, editorial shoots, brand showcases, outfit reveals, and style lookbooks. We believe fashion comes alive through people, not just clothes. Our 7,000+ subscribers tune in specifically to see models, making our audience highly targeted and purchase-ready for fashion brands. Based in India, our content bridges local fashion culture with global aesthetics — giving international brands direct access to India's young, style-conscious audience.
"Every outfit tells a story — we find the model who tells it best."
